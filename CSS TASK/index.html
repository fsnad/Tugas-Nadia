All Day Project

All Day Project is a South Korean mixed-gender idol group consisting five members. They make their debut on June 23, 2025 under The Black Label Entertainment. Their debut single "Famous" become a hit, as they combine male and female dynamics in one team. They always update their activity on Instagram, Twitter, TikTok, Facebook, Threads, Weibo, and YouTube.
